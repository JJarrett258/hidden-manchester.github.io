The cross and cenotaph were moved in 2015 to make way for new tram lines for the second city crossing, supported by a large concrete slab so that trams could run over the crypt without disturbing it. Read more in this BBC News article: Unearthing Manchester's hidden crypt and secret cemetery.

You can see photographs of the church exterior, the interior, sealed vaults, underground caskets and a passage adjoining the vaults in Manchester’s Local Image Collection.
